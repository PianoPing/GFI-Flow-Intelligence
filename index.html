GL framework

Measure delivery before failure becomes normal.

GL Framework helps leaders identify where delay, friction, and badly sequenced process design are destroying value.

For Government For Enterprise
Core claim
The denominator matters.
Many systems measure budgets and outputs but ignore the burden of time delay and cognitive friction. That is where delivery quality collapses.
Diagnostic ratio
GL = (Fs × Vn) / (Pd × Cf)
Fs = Flow Success Rate · Vn = Strategic Value · Pd = Pain Duration · Cf = Cognitive Friction

What GL measures

GL is a diagnostic ratio for implementation efficiency. It highlights where process design, sequencing, and friction undermine throughput, trust, and value.

01

Delivery quality

Not whether a policy exists on paper, but whether people can move through the system with acceptable speed, clarity, and completion.

02

Administrative friction

Identifies where documentation burden, sequence overload, and fragmented process design create hidden waste.

03

Executive signal

Produces a decision-ready frame that leaders can use in briefings, audits, process review, and strategic prioritization.
